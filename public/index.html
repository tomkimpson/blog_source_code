About

Hey, I’m Tom ✌️

I am currently a Research Fellow at the University of Melbourne and OzGrav.

My main research interests are in strong-field gravity and gravitational wave astrophysics.

I was previously a postdoc in computational physics at the University of Oxford where I research reduced precision float arithmetic, stochastic methods and machine learning techniques applied to climate simulations. I got my PhD in theoretical astrophysics in 2021 from University College London, within the in the Mullard Space Science Laboratory. During this period I also spent some time at the Perimeter Institute for Theoretical Physics as Visiting Graduate Fellow.

In addition to academic research, I have spent some time doing cool stuff in applied ML and Data Science @ Mind Foundry, Revolut, Shopify and Pace.
